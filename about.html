Amazon FBA Service and Development Agency

How does the process work and how do we manage to serve over 150 customers globally?

Our way

We are a young and digital Amazon FBA sourcing & development agency.

Our core tasks are to support existing Amazon FBA e-commerce companies as well as start-ups in German & English speaking countries in expanding and finding profitable products as well as fully setting up the Amazon FBA Seller Central account.
With over 150+ clients in the DACH region and the USA, we are one of the fastest growing companies in our industry.
Our goal is to give our clients the winning advantage in their market & niche segment to build a real substantial & sustainable e-commerce brand. With great creativity, passion for the process & helping hands here in Potsdam (DE) we contribute to this!

Sourcing and importing into the Amazon FBA warehouse

Creating and setting up the Amazon FBA seller account

Creating the overall product storefront & branded content
